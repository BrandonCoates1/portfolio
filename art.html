Erika Chiba

Erika Chiba is a character from a Japanese show called
Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei.

This was drawn back in 2015, and was back when I was
just starting to draw. I only did a 'neat' sketch of Erika
Chiba, as she is probably my favorite character from
the show.

Back then, I definitely wasn't confident at all
and only really did neat sketches, as I am pretty sure
I didn't even know about line art.

This drawing is definitely one of the ones that got me
into art as my interest grew from the ones before this.
I look at this now wanting to redraw it and see what that
would look like now, and to compare each other to see if
an improvement in my art has been made.

Tatsumi

Tatsumi is the main character from a Japanese show called
Akame ga Kill!

This was done back in 2018, and was back when I quit art a
few months in. I drew Tatsumi because I found this image
from the show and I wanted to try and colour.

I think this definitely looks unfinished in some areas but
it was a good effort seeing as this is one of the first few
art works I have done with colour.

Looking back at this now, it makes me want to continue to
develop my colouring skills, as too be honest, I have lost
motivation to do any sort of colouring. I might even go back
and re-do this.

Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno

Sasuke and Sakura are a married couple from a Japanese
show called Naruto.

I'm not sure when exactly this was done, but it was
sometime in 2018. I drew this because Naruto is one
of my favorite shows, and Sasuke and Sakura are
characters I have never drawn before.

I went for a merge of the 2 characters as I saw similar
art works online. I also experimented with line
thickness, which looking back now makes the art work
look good, but with thinner lines it would look better.

I also see now that the shading is undeveloped, and
would look better if added more depth.
