About Me

Hello, my name is Brandon. I enjoy reading, playing video games, drawing and coding.
Coding is a new passion of mine, but probably the most fun out of everything I do. I
enjoy thinking of solutions and how I can do something, then actually doing the thing
I want. I believe it is the same with drawing, I can go design and creatively think
of solutions that I then produce.
Overall, I enjoy using my brain to achieve high standards of work and love fixing and
solving problems.
